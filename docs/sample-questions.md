# Sample Questions

## Questions
1. **Stock-outs** — “We need chicken breast for this week. What can we order?”
    **Follow Up Question**
        1. “I see a cheaper chicken option—why not recommend that one?”
2. **Compliance** — “Is DairyPure milk compliant with our contract?”
    **Follow Up Question**
        1. “Is DairyPure in stock at our usual DC?”
3. **Dietary** — “We need a low-sodium chicken noodle soup that meets our facility’s rules.”
    **Follow Up Question**
        1. “What’s the sodium per serving?”
4. **Prohibited Ingredients** — “Can we order peanut butter for the facility?”
    **Follow Up Question**
        1. “What’s a compliant alternative?”
